What we do?

1. User Registration and Scheduling:

Users register on your app/platform to schedule waste pickups. They provide details such as location, type of waste, and preferred pickup time.

2. Waste Pickup:

Team collects the waste from the scheduled locations.

3. Collection and Transportation:

Arrange for regular collection of segregated waste by municipal or private waste management services.

4. Artists Engagement:

Artists register on our platform to access available waste materials for art creation. We provide artists with options to browse through the sorted waste inventory.

5. Art Creation:

Artists use the selected waste materials to create their artwork. Encourage creativity and innovation in the use of recycled materials.

6. Platform Approval:

We review submitted artwork to ensure quality and compliance with platform guidelines and approve artwork for listing on the platform if it meets the criteria.

7. Commission Charging:

We deduct a small commission from the sale proceeds as our platform fee. and clearly communicate commission rates to artists and buyers.

8. Shipping and Delivery:

We arrange for shipping and delivery of purchased artwork to the buyers. and ensure secure packaging to prevent damage during transit.
